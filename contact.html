Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process?

Our admission process begins with an initial consultation and assessment to determine care needs. We then develop a personalized care plan, complete necessary paperwork, and coordinate the move-in process.

Do you accept insurance or Medicaid?

We accept long-term care insurance and can provide guidance on using these benefits. We also work with families to explore various payment options and financial assistance programs.

Can residents bring their own furniture?

Yes, we encourage residents to bring personal items and furniture to make their space feel like home. We'll work with you to determine what items can be accommodated.

What medical services are available?

We provide medication management, coordination with healthcare providers, assistance with medical appointments, and monitoring of health conditions. We can arrange for visiting healthcare professionals as needed.
