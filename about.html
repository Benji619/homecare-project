About Bethel Family Senior Care Home

Founded in December 2024, we are dedicated to providing exceptional care in a warm, home-like environment.

Our Mission

At Bethel Family Senior Care Home, our mission is to provide compassionate, personalized care that empowers each resident to live with dignity, comfort, and joy. We are dedicated to creating a warm, supportive home environment where individuals feel valued and respected. Through attentive, professional care and a family-centered approach, we aim to enhance the quality of life for each resident, promoting independence while ensuring their safety, health, and overall well-being.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a trusted and welcoming home where every resident experiences a life of comfort, dignity, and belonging. We strive to create a supportive and joyful environment where compassionate care enhances each individual's well-being and independence. At Bethel Family Home Care, we aim to set a standard for quality adult family home care, fostering a community where residents and their families feel embraced, respected, and truly at home.

Our Care Team

Our dedicated team consists of highly qualified caregivers with extensive experience in senior care. Each team member is carefully selected for their compassion, reliability, and commitment to providing excellent care.

Our Qualifications

  • Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) certification
  • Home Care Aide (HCA) certification
  • CPR and First Aid certified
  • Specialized training in memory care
  • Medication management certification
  • Ongoing professional development
